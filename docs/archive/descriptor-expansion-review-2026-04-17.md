## Series templates (apply to all matching titles)

One line covers every mainline entry. Faster to maintain than writing a near-identical blurb per numbered sequel.

- **Civilization (any mainline: Civ IV, V, VI, VII)** → `"Just one more turn. You will say this at 3 AM. You will be lying."`
- **FIFA / EA Sports FC (any yearly release)** → `"The yearly one. You know what it is. You know why you bought it."`
- **Souls-likes (non-FromSoft: Lords of the Fallen, Lies of P, Nioh, Mortal Shell, The Surge, Wo Long)** → `"A Souls-like. It wants to kill you. You want to die trying."`

## Notes

- **Skipped (score-tier likely fits):** Most annual sports roster updates beyond the series-template hits. If a game already scores 85+ on Metacritic and is genre-typical, the existing scored fallbacks read fine. Avoided padding with "Madden 24 is football again" type lines where the series template covers it.
- **Series coverage uncertainty:** The Souls-like template is deliberately loose. If someone owns Elden Ring + Lies of P + Nioh 2, they get the Elden Ring curated line for one and the Souls-like template for the others. Consider whether Nioh deserves its own curated entry — it is mechanically distinct enough.
- **GTA V** is Tier A despite being everywhere because roughly every Steam library on earth has it. Worth a custom line.
- **Minecraft / Fortnite / Roblox tier** — Roblox was omitted intentionally; the audience overlap with Inventory Full users is thin.
- **Pokemon template caveat:** If a specific Pokemon game breaks from the formula (Legends Arceus already has a custom line, Legends Z-A might warrant one), promote it out of the template.
- **FF XIV edge case** — it is an MMO with no real end. The line acknowledges the sub reality. If Brady wants a more protective line for subscription commitment avoidance, suggest swapping.
- **Dwarf Fortress phrasing** — "Losing is fun" is the community's own slogan, not a shame jab. Keeping it.
- **Voice check passed:** no "tonight", no em dashes for pauses, no "delve/tapestry/journey", no "whether you're", no triple adjective lists, no "that's not X, that's Y" reframes beyond a couple isolated uses. Sentence lengths vary per line.
