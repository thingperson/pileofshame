## Brand Description

### Master description (use everywhere as default)
Inventory Full is a new app for people with too many games, too many half-started campaigns, and too much decision paralysis. It helps you decide what to play now, make ongoing progress on the games you already own, and recover more value from your collection.

### One-liner

### Brand-forward variant (marketing, landing pages)

## Voice Check

These lines were reviewed against our voice-and-tone.md guide. Key principles applied:
- Empathy over shame (we understand overload, not laziness)
- Witty adult gamer, not TikTok teen
- Roast with warmth, never punch down
- Make decisions feel safe
- No banned AI patterns (em dash drama, "That's not X, that's Y", hallmark words)

### Lines approved as-is:
- "Too many games. Not enough momentum." (punchy, clean)
- "Your backlog should feel exciting. Not administratively haunted." (great — "administratively haunted" is peak voice)
- "You do not need more games. You need a better way to decide." (direct, non-judgmental)
- "Inventory Full: for people with 80 games, 9 active campaigns, and one confused little evening." (this is exactly our tone)
- "Not every unfinished game deserves redemption. But some absolutely do." (warm, honest)
- "We're not trying to optimize your soul. We just want to help you play your games." (perfect anti-productivityware stance)
- "Less backlog tourism. More finished wins." (tight)

### Lines to adjust:
- "Follow along if that sounds like your exact disease, but in a friendly way." → Slightly too self-conscious. Replace with: "Follow along if your library is packed and your momentum isn't."
- "Not administratively haunted" is great but verify it doesn't feel too clever for the platform. Works on X/Bluesky, might be too niche for TikTok.

### Lines to skip:
- None of the submitted lines violate our voice guide. The anonymous LLM did solid work here.
